About
Search for English terms and find the Estonian translations. In contrast to other online dictionaries, the descriptions are enriched with additional information. For instance the singular nominative, singular genitive and singular partitive for nouns are displayed. Also the rules for forming other cases are shown.

This dictionaries combines two online services of the Eesti Keele Institue (EKI, The Institue of the Estonian Language): The [IES] English-Estonian MT dictionary and the [ÕS] Eesti õigekeelsussõnaraamat ÕS 2013.
